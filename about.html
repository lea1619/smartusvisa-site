Seth Finberg, Esq.

Immigration Attorney

Georgia Bar Licensed Finberg Firm PLLC Eagle Immigration Law South Florida

Seth Finberg is a licensed immigration attorney based in South Florida. He is the principal attorney at Eagle Immigration Law (eagleimmigrationlaw.com) and a member of Finberg Firm PLLC. Seth is licensed to practice law in the State of Georgia.

Seth focuses his practice on employment-based immigration, including H-1B, O-1A, L-1, and TN work visas, as well as employment-based green cards including EB-1A extraordinary ability and EB-2 NIW National Interest Waiver self-petitions.

He has assisted professionals from India, China, and dozens of other countries in navigating the U.S. immigration system to advance their careers and achieve permanent residency.
