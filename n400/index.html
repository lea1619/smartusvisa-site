Is This Right for You?

Who this service is for

✓ Good fit — Standard Path

  • Green card holder preparing for naturalization
  • Clear eligibility (5-year or 3-year rule)
  • No extended absences outside the U.S.
  • No criminal, tax, or Selective Service issues
  • Want guided support, not a blank form
  • Prefer bilingual (EN/中文) assistance

✕ May need attorney consultation first

  • Single trip outside U.S. over 6 months
  • Arrests, charges, or conviction history
  • Tax compliance issues or outstanding obligations
  • Selective Service registration concerns
  • Prior removal, denial, or immigration violations
  • Other complex legal background
How It Works

From eligibility check to attorney review

  • 1

    Online Eligibility Pre-Screen

    Complete a short intake to determine whether your case qualifies for the standard automated path. Takes about 5 minutes.

  • 2

    Guided Information Collection

    The system walks you through biographical details, address history, travel history, marital and family information, employment, and background questions — in English or Chinese.

  • 3

    Structured Draft Preparation

    Your responses are organized into a structured package for N-400 preparation and legal review.

  • 4

    Attorney Review

    A licensed attorney at Finberg Firm PLLC reviews your information, flags potential issues, and requests clarification where needed.

  • 5

    Filing Preparation & Next-Step Guidance

    After review, you receive a filing-ready package and guidance on biometrics, interview preparation, and follow-up requests.
